Tetris 2012
------------
A Tetris remake.

Controls
---------
W / Up - Rotate the figure.
A / Left - Move the figure left.
D / Right - Move the figure right.
S / Down - Move the figure down.
Space - Drop the figure.
G - Turn on/off the ghost of the figure.
F1 - Help.
F12 - Take a screenshot.

Scoring
--------
1 line at once = 10 x level
2 lines at once = 30 x level
3 lines at once = 60 x level
4 lines at once = 100 x level

Notes
------
If there is no sound in the game install oalinst.exe located in the same folder.
